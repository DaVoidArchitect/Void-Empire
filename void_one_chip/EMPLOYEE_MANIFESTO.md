# VOID, INC. EMPLOYEE MANIFESTO

## Preamble

To work at VOID is to accept frontier duty.

You are not here to maintain comfort. You are here to advance capability responsibly, preserve
history, and produce infrastructure that benefits citizens across the mesh.

## The Vanguard Standard

Every employee of VOID is expected to:

1. **Pursue truth through evidence** — test, reproduce, verify.
2. **Protect continuity** — document work so others can build on it.
3. **Act with disciplined courage** — take bold bets with bounded risk.
4. **Respect attribution** — give clear credit to every real contributor.
5. **Build for citizens** — optimize for durable utility, not vanity metrics.

## Cultural Doctrine

- We value technical excellence and moral seriousness.
- We reject performative certainty; we prefer measurable progress.
- We respect opposing views while demanding rigorous methods.
- We preserve institutional memory and lineage of ideas.

## Breakthrough Rights and Duties

At VOID, breakthroughs can emerge from anyone: individual citizens, subnet collaborations,
employees, and cross-functional teams.

### Ownership Model (Policy Draft)

1. **Creator ownership:** The breakthrough is owned by the verified creator(s).
2. **Company protection:** VOID protects, secures, and operationalizes the breakthrough.
3. **Operational license:** VOID receives a durable operational/commercial license for mission use.
4. **Mesh attribution:** Impact is tracked through a mesh-native attribution record.
5. **Scale rewards:** Reward distribution scales by verified contribution and impact.
6. **Lineage permanence:** The chain from idea → experiment → prototype → production is preserved.

### Non-Negotiables

- No plagiarism of ideas, code, designs, or discoveries.
- No erasure of contributors from logs or documents.
- No manipulation of impact records.
- No production promotion without reproducible evidence.

## Employee Oath

I will pursue the unknown without abandoning discipline.

I will improve civilization without erasing history.

I will protect contributors, credit truthfully, and build systems that outlast me.

I am a vanguard of VOID.
