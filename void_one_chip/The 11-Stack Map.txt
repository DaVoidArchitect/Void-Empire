These stacks are etched into the origin_v_grand_core.sv logic gates.

Stack 01 (Physical): The silicon die itself, manufactured by the foundry.

Stack 02 (Hard-Law): The founder_share and system_share arithmetic logic units (ALUs).

Stack 03 (Bio-Latch): The bio_entropy_in pin and comparison logic.

Stack 04 (Fractal NoC): The 1.0T TPS throughput enabled by the AXI-Stream non-blocking architecture.

2. THE SOVEREIGN INTERFACE (Hardware Hooks)

These stacks are enabled by the pins on the chip, forcing software to comply.

Stack 05 (Storage): The hardware_storage_key output. Data is encrypted by biology, not passwords.

Stack 06 (Network): The core_authorized signal. If the chip is tampered with, this signal cuts the network connection physically.

Stack 07 (Pulse): The pulse_velocity_accumulator register. It tracks economic impact at the hardware level.

These stacks read the hardware registers to build the "Smart Nation."

Stack 09 (Governance): The governance_weight register. Voting power is read directly from this silicon register.

Stack 10 (Civilization): The civilization_state telemetry. This broadcasts the node's status to the global MMO map.

Stack 11 (Singularity): The sum total of a compliant, interconnected mesh where is_founder is recognized.
